About Pixcy Studios

Founded by Pradeep Bernard, Pixcy Studios is a premier photography and videography service based in Chennai, specializing in capturing life's most precious moments.

With years of experience in wedding photography, pre-wedding shoots, baby showers, maternity sessions, corporate events, and more, we bring creativity, professionalism, and passion to every project.

We serve clients across South India, combining traditional techniques with modern cinematic storytelling to create timeless memories you'll cherish forever.
